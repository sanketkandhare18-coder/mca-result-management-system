About the Institution

Genba Sopanrao Moze College Of Engineering
of Commerce, Arts & Science

Genba Sopanrao Moze College of Engineering (GSMCOE), Balewadi, located in Pune, Maharashtra, was established in 1999. The institution was established under the umbrella of the Genba Sopanrao Moze Trust and has been a part of the Pune technical education landscape for over two decades.Key Historical & Institutional Highlights:Establishment: The Balewadi campus was founded in 1999.Affiliation: The college is affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU).Accreditation: GSMCOE is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with a "B+" grade.Approvals: The institution is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and recognized by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra State.Location: Situated in the Balewadi area of Pune, it is close to the Balewadi Stadium..

Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and re-accredited with a B++ grade by NAAC, the college has grown from a Gujarati-minority institution into a vibrant, inclusive academic community with over 2,500 students and approximately 80 experienced faculty members.

The college offers UG and PG programmes in Science, Arts, Commerce & Management, including MCA, BCA, BSc, BA, BBA, BCom, MCA and MSc. Top recruiters include Cognizant, Infosys, HCL, Capgemini and ICICI Bank.
